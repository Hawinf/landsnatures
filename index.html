Landnatures Indonesia is the leading supplier of products from Indonesia. We are a company engaged in manufacturing various konjac products with high-quality materials. We process more than 1,500 hectares of Konjac plantations, spread across several places in Central Java Indonesia. In the begining our company was focussing only production and selling our product in local market here in Indonesia, along with time our product better known and spread in Asia. Earlier our product was Konjac Chips and Glucommanan Powder, along with time we inovate our product and improving the product such as Glucommanan Powder Sun Dried, Glucommanan Oven Dried, Konjac Rice.

Landnatures Indonesia has served customers from all over the world and it has become our top priority to meet our customers’ satisfaction and expectations.
